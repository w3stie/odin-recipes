Lasagna

Lasagna also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can alsorefer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and seasonings and spices.

Everyone loves a good lasagna, right? It's a great way to a crowd and a perfect dish to bring to a potluck. It freezes well. It reheats well. Leftovers will keep you happy for days
